Number of Trees in Utrecht Choropleth Map

Map Description

This map reveals the distribution of trees between the districts in the Province of Utrecht. It enables the comparison of the tree density between the districts to manage and work towards improving on indicators under SDG 15: Life on Land.

It states: "Protect, restore and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, and halt and reverse land degradation and halt biodiversity loss". The Goal has 12 targets to be achieved by 2030. Progress towards targets will be measured by 14 indicators.

Map Making Process

The map was made using vector data on the number and type of trees in each District in Utrecht publicly available in The Netherlands. The vector data was then transformed into a Choropleth Map on ArcGIS Pro to provide a visual understanding about the distrubution of trees between Districts in Utrecht.
